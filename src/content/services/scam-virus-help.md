You may notice:

- Strange advertisements
- Your browser opening random websites

## Common Scams We Help With

### Fake Microsoft Support Scams

A popup claims your computer is infected and tells you to call a phone number.

### Remote Access Scams

Someone asks you to install remote access software so they can “fix” your computer.

### Fake Antivirus Warnings

A website pretends your computer has severe infections and pressures you to buy software.

### Email & Banking Scams

Suspicious emails asking for passwords, payments, or personal information.

---

## What To Do If You Think You Have Been Scammed

### Do Not Panic

Many scams rely on fear and urgency.

### Disconnect From The Internet

If possible, disconnect your computer from Wi-Fi or unplug the network cable.

### Stop Entering Information

Do not provide passwords, banking information, or remote access.

### Contact Us

The sooner the issue is checked, the better.
